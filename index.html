About Edu Connect

Edu Connect is an Android mobile application designed to connect learners with valuable educational resources and tutors. Upload books and documents you believe will be helpful, categorize them, and share them with the community. Tutors can also offer lessons and assist students to help them succeed. Edu Connect makes learning collaborative and accessible to everyone.
